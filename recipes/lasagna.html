Lasagna

Description

John Chandler submitted this recipe 20 years ago on allrecipes.com, which became immensely popular and is now considered the "World's Best Lasagna" on the website. This recipe could be considered simple relative to other recipes, but easily customizable due to that.

Ingredients

  • Meat - consists of Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion - diced onions cooked with the meat.
  • Garlic - two cloves of garlic paired with the onions.
  • Canned tomatoes - one can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar - two tablespons of sugar.
  • Spices and seasonings - fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese - paramesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese.
  • Egg - one egg.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce by cooking the meat and later putting in diced onions and garlic.
  2. Cook the noodles in salted water.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture of cheese.
  4. Layer the lasagna with meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, parmesan cheese, and repeat where you will top with the lasting paremsan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
